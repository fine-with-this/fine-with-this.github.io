Fine With This


Fine With This is a work-in-progress and collaboration between Taylor Hokanson, Kay Dartt, and Stephen Lee, and is supported by a Faculty Development Grant from Columbia College Chicago. Inspired in part by KC Green of “This is Fine” fame, FWT will be comprised of a series of large (>12″), cast iron letters. These casts will be plumbed with propane, so they can burst into flame on command. FWT is designed to be rearrangeable, allowing us to change the text in response to the multitude of sociopolitical crisis competing for our attention in 2020.
